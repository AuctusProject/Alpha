Welcome to Auctus Tournament

Auctus Tournament is a blockchain-based simulated investment challenge. It is designed to serve as a fun and interactive experience to educate users about how AUC token will work.

By registering an account and joining our telegram, everyone receives 100 TestAUC and $1000 virtual cash (We're talking virtual cash, not real money. This is strictly a simulation.)

There are two different players categories: Advisors and Investors. Advisors can earn AUC by recommending portfolios with a expected return associated to it. Investors can invest in portfolios recommended by advisors using AUC.

The AUC advisory fee is only paid if the portfolio performance is above the expected return.
